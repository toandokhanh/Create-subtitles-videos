How do I add subtitles to a video?

560

You have 3 ways to add subtitles to your videos: manually import them, use the auto-capturing tool, or upload a subtitle file. If you need help, use live chat for support.

How do I edit Subtitles?

259

After creating subtitles and you'll see a list of text boxes with clickable, editable text that updates in real-time during video playback. Each text box has a start and end time so you can control when each caption appears and how long it is displayed.


How to change the display style of subtitles?

106

All you have to do is click 'Subtitles' on the sidebar, then press 'Style'. This will allow you to choose the font, size, spacing between letters, line height, background color, alignment, bold, italics, and more.

How do I translate subtitles (automatically)?

20

You can easily translate your subtitles into over 100 different languages with just one click. Simply add your subtitles in the "Subtitles" section and click on "Translate". Choose the language you wish to translate to and let the magic begin!

Benefits of Subtitling Your Videos

Accessibility

Subtitles make your videos accessible to a wider audience, including those who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Subtitles can help viewers understand the content of your videos more easily, especially if the language spoken is not their first language.

SEO

Subtitles can help improve your video's search engine optimization by making it easier for search engines to index your content.

Noise Reduction Algorithm

Our subtitle generator allows users to choose a noise reduction algorithm to increase the accuracy of subtitles.
